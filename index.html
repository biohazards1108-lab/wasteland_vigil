About the game

Wasteland Vigil is a top down survival zombie game set in a devastated world of rust, ash, and broken concrete. You wake alone in the ruins, scavenging for supplies, building makeshift defenses, and pushing deeper into the unknown to uncover what’s left of humanity.

Designed and built by a solo developer, Wasteland Vigil focuses on atmosphere, tension, and the feeling of scraping by one day at a time in a world that doesn’t care if you make it.

Features

Dynamic survival

Manage hunger, thirst, stamina, and health as you navigate a hostile wasteland.

Scavenge & craft

Search abandoned buildings, vehicles, and ruins for scrap to craft weapons, tools, and upgrades.

Base building

Claim a corner of the ruins and fortify it with barricades, traps, and workbenches.

Day & night cycle

Travel by day, hide or hunt by night—each cycle changes visibility, danger, and opportunities.

Hostile encounters

Face desperate survivors and other threats that roam the wasteland after dark.

Progression

Upgrade your gear, improve your shelter, and push further into the dead city with each run.
